# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We — contributors and maintainers — pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language, and giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the wider community; showing empathy toward others.
- Keeping technical disagreement technical — argue the code, the spec, and the model, not the person.

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments; personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission.
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported **privately** to the
maintainers via the repository's **Security → Report a vulnerability** channel (it opens a private report only
maintainers can see). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly; the maintainers will
respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

Maintainers who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent
repercussions as determined by the project's other maintainers.
